Our Product: SureSmile Aligners

WHAT ARE CLEAR ALIGNERS?

Clear aligners are transparent, removable trays that gradually shift your teeth. Made from BPA-free, medical-grade thermoplastic, they are safe, stain-resistant, and comfortable.

HOW THEY WORK

  1. Consultation & 3D Scan
  2. Treatment Planning with MaxFlex Software
  3. Production of Custom Aligner Series
  4. Regular Progress Checks
  5. Retainers Post-Treatment

Product Benefits

  1. Discreet Appearance – nearly invisible
  2. Customized Per Case
  3. Comfortable & Removable
  4. No Food Restrictions
